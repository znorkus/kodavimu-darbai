WOLF

The Great Plains wolf (Canis lupus nubilus), also known as the buffalo wolf or loafer, is a subspecies of gray wolf that once extended throughout the Great Plains, from southern Manitoba and Saskatchewan in Canada southward to northern Texas in the United States.

  • Scientific name: Canis Lupus
  • Average height: 80 to 85 cm
  • Mass: 30-80kg
  • Lifespan: 5 - 6 years

Wolf is the largest predator of the dog family groups, colloquially called packs.
